MINHS - SCHOOL GRADING SYSTEM
One-Page Case Study

Problem:
Teachers were manually computing and verifying grades, creating delays
and introducing avoidable human error during submission windows.

Role:
C# Developer and Database Implementer.

Process:
- Designed MySQL-backed data model for class, subject, and grading records.
- Implemented grade computation logic in C#.
- Added validation checks for grade entry and retrieval workflows.

Constraints:
- Needed to align with school grading policies.
- Needed to run on available school hardware.

Outcome:
- Reduced repeated manual computation work.
- Improved turnaround by roughly 40% in internal testing scenarios.

What I Learned:
Validation-first data modeling is essential in education systems where
accuracy and consistency are non-negotiable.
