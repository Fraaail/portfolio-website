AUTOMATED BARANGAY CLEARANCE SYSTEM
One-Page Case Study

Problem:
Manual transcription of resident request data caused processing bottlenecks
and increased clerical mistakes.

Role:
Systems Developer.

Process:
- Connected Google Form inputs to the application workflow.
- Automated PDF clearance generation.
- Standardized print-ready document output.

Constraints:
- Needed format compliance for official document templates.
- Needed resilient behavior for unstable local connectivity.

Outcome:
- Reduced duplicate encoding tasks.
- Improved request processing consistency for recurring request flows.
- Internal team testing indicated ~50% less repeated manual entry effort.

What I Learned:
Automation is most effective when it supports familiar staff workflows
instead of forcing abrupt process changes.
